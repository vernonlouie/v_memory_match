About the Card Match Game

The game consists of 9 pairs of cards. You must match the pairs is in the game of concentration. You flip one card (by clicking) and it remains flipped. You then flip another card to see if it matches. If it matches, then you have discovered a matching pair and the two cards remain face up. If it doesn't match, then the 2 cards go face down. You then proceed to do another turn. Once you have matched all 9 pairs, then the game ends and you can check your statistics (stats), which measure attempts and accuracy. You can reset the game at any time by clicking on the "reset game" button.
